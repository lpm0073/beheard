Introducing: Virtual Protests

Description

Web & mobile app that enables you to express your opinion about any current event to "the goverment". the app facilitates virtual demonstrations that are formed on-the-fly by analyzing your free form text message (anything from a sentence or two to a literal manifesto) + an optional selfie image for context to correctly classify your issue and then combine it with others; delivering a complete consolidated analysis to the appropriate public official(s) at the federal, state and local levels.

Use case

a public policy matter is bothering you. you navigate to this new awesome site and impulsively send a freeform text message + an optional selfie or group selfie to "the powers that be". the app will do the following:

  1. analyze the text of your message for tone and context, identifying relationships to recent political events and policy discussions to determine if this is a federal, state or local matter. automatically categorize your message with dynamically-generated tag clouds.
  2. use geocoding to determine the exact public officials who influence the matter
  3. analyze the image for faces, demographic information, and tone. analyze for context in the image that might reinforce the message (location, gestures, text on signs / banners, etc)
  4. provide you with the option to either send the message anonymously, or to sign the message, including as much or as little personal information as you prefer
  5. provide an option to share your message on social media channels to invite others to join (sign onto) your message
  6. deliver your message to the appropriate public official(s). officials actually receive a regularly updated and fully digested executive summary report of consolidated analysis of constituents' sentiment on your issue and how it is trending.
