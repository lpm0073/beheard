Lean Canvas

The Problem

Top 3 Problems

What is the crucial problem faced by your customers? Capture their central frustration.

Existing alternatives

Define one clear, direct competitor. Consider the other ways customers can address their problems. What products or servicds exist as alternatives to what we're offering?

The Solution

Top 3 Solutions

What is our solution to customers' problem? Present the defining elements our service. What makes it the top tool for addressing customers' needs?

Key Metrics

Key activities we measure

How will we track consumers engagement, excitement and usage of our product?

Unique Value Proposition

A Clear and compelling message

This message should explain what we do, how we are different and why we are worth investing in. What is our promise to consumers? Zero in on the heart of our service and highlight what stands our about the service we provide.

High level concept

How does our service fit into the bigger picture? Where does it fall in the grand scheme of things?

Unfair Advantage

Our differentiator

How do we stand out from competitors? What puts us ahead of the pack? Why should consumers have confidence in our service above others?

Channels

Path to customers

How will we interact with consumers, inform them of our developments and services? Print ads, social media platforms, promotional events, or even word of mouth?

Customer Segments

Target Customers

Who can we help? Identify 3 or 4 user personas we envision turning to us for solutions.

Early Adopters

Define the specific characteristics of our early adopter customers.

Cost Structure

Customer acquisition costs, distribution costs, hosting, people, etc.

What will it cost to launch and maintain this business? Consider each stage of our startup from creating a web site and acquiring users to hiring employees and producing goods to marketing our service and delivering to customers.

Revenue Streams

Revenue model, lifetime value, gross margin, etc.

What monetary sources will fuel this company? How will we generate income? Generate a pricing model for our service and then highlight other sources of revenue: ad sales, subscription fees, or other sales.
